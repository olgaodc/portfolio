About Me

Hello world! I am Olga Odincova, a Front-End Developer specializing in React and Node.js. I find great joy in tackling the challenges that come with learning a new skill set.

Before becoming a Front-End Developer, I worked in various fields. Most recently, I worked as a Junior Chemist in Thermo Fisher. However, my passion lies in working behind the computer, constantly learning and exploring new technologies. For this reason, I graduated from Vilnius TECH with a Master’s degree in Innovation and Technology Communication. The education helped me deepen my knowledge about innovations and improved my communication skills.

I was always in search for a suitable profession for myself. One day I decided to change my life and start my programming journey. During the course at the Code Academy, I became even more convinced that programming is the field where I want to show my full potential. I am really proud that I decided to change my career. One of the things I love most about programming are the endless possibilities for creating something new and useful. I am particularly interested in user experience and design, and I strive to create websites that are not only functional but also aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly.

I❤️Programming and I am really excited to see where my programming journey takes me. I am eager to work with others to create innovative and impactful projects.
